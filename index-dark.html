About the Workshop

Despite much progress in AI, closing the "efficiency gap" — the orders-of-magnitude difference between human and machine intelligence — remains one of the grand challenges of our time.
This workshop brings together world experts in the computational modeling of human cognition, alongside AI/ML and robotics researchers, to share their research and explore implications for closing this gap.

Talks span three themes: how humans and machines learn, how they plan and reason, and how they perceive the world.
